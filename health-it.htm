The Health IT Opportunity

Health Information Technology is one of the fastest growing industries in the country with Atlanta regarded by many as its capital. With this event we would like to provide information about Health IT, its many career opportunities and Atlanta's impact on the industry.

The core of the event will consist of a featured speaker who will give a general presentation on Health IT followed by one or two representatives from companies in the industry who will discuss their specific involvement in the industry. There will be opportunities for mingling and networking before and after the presentations.
