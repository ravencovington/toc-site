Georgia Day of Code

The Technologists of Color helped to introduce approximately 150 students at Lindley Sixth Grade Academy to computer programming during the Georgia Day of Code. The program coincided with both the international Hour of Code program organized by Code.org and the nation-wide Computer Science Week. The students took a self-guided tutorial introducing them to the fundamentals of programming; including repeat-loops, conditionals and basic algorithms. Students will also be encouraged to work together to complete the tutorial.
